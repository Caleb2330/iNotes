# Privacy Policy for iNotes

**Last Updated:** December 12, 2024

## Introduction

Welcome to iNotes. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Data Stored Locally on Your Device

iNotes is designed with privacy in mind. All your notes and app preferences are stored **locally on your device**. We do not collect, transmit, or store your notes on external servers.

The app stores the following data locally:
- **Notes:** All notes you create, including text content and metadata (creation date, modification date)
- **App Preferences:** Your theme settings and onboarding completion status
- **Folders/Categories:** Any organizational structures you create for your notes

### Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do not collect personal identification information
- We do not collect location data
- We do not collect usage analytics
- We do not access your contacts, camera, or other device features
- We do not transmit any of your notes or data to external servers

## Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms provided by the operating system. Your notes remain on your device and are not backed up to our servers.

**Important:** If you uninstall the app or clear app data, your notes will be permanently deleted as they are only stored locally.

## Third-Party Services

iNotes does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Your data stays on your device.

## Children's Privacy

iNotes does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The app is suitable for users of all ages as it does not collect or transmit personal data.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. You are advised to review this privacy policy periodically for any changes.

## Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information:
- **Access:** You can view all your notes within the app
- **Deletion:** You can delete individual notes or all data by uninstalling the app
- **Portability:** Your data is stored on your device and accessible to you
